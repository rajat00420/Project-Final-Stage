Ferrari

Ferrari is an Italian Company which has produced sports cars since 1947 but traces back to 1929 when ENZO Rerrari formed the Scudera Ferrari racing team. Unlike many similar Yet independent comapnies, Fiat Group-Owned Ferrari continued to thrive after the death of its charismatic founder and is today one of the most successful sports car compaies in the world. In January 2016, Ferrari has offically split off from its former parent company Fiat Chrysler Automobiles.

Jaguar

Jaguar Cars is a brand of Jaguar Land Rover, a British multinational car manufacturer headquartered in Whitley, Coventry, England, owned by Tata Motors since 2008.Jaguar cars today are designed in Jaguar Land Rover’s engineering centres at the Whitley plant in Coventry and at their Gaydon site in Warwickshire, and are manufactured in Jaguar’s Castle Bromwich assembly plant in Birmingham with some manufacturing expected to take place in the Solihull plant.
